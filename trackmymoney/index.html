Privacy Policy for Track My Money

Effective Date: 10 April 2026

    We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers (like Android Advertising ID) and location data to show personalized ads. This helps us keep the app free for you.

      For more details, please read their policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

      Please read their policy: Firebase Privacy & Security

      Learn more here: Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  4. Local Data Security
    Your sensitive financial data never leaves your device unless you manually perform a 'Backup' to your own storage. We provide an 'App Lock' feature to help you keep this data secure.

    These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
